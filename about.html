What Does it Mean to Go Green?

Going green is about making choices that are better for the environment. This can include things like:

  • Reducing our consumption of energy and resources.
  • Recycling and composting.
  • Buying products that are made from sustainable materials.
  • Supporting businesses that are committed to environmental responsibility.
  • Making more sustainable choices such as riding a bike instead of driving.

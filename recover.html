Welcome to the Recover Page!

Explore the essential role of resource recovery in sustainable waste management.

What is Recovery?

Resource recovery involves reclaiming valuable materials from waste to prevent them from ending up in landfills. This process conserves resources and supports sustainable practices.

Why Recovery Matters

Recovery reduces waste volume, minimizes environmental impact, and provides alternative resources like energy and raw materials.

Types of Recovery

Energy Recovery

Waste-to-energy technologies convert waste into usable energy, reducing reliance on fossil fuels.

Material Recovery

Processes like shredding and sorting help reclaim valuable materials like metals and plastics.

Organic Recovery

Methods such as composting and anaerobic digestion turn organic waste into valuable resources for agriculture.

  • Support Recycling Facilities: Use and support local recycling and recovery facilities.
  • Advocate for Policies: Promote policies that improve resource recovery.
  • Educate and Spread the Word: Help raise awareness about the benefits of resource recovery.
